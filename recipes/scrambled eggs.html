Scrambled eggs

Add some cottage cheese to your scrambled eggs for an easy high-protein breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup 2% cottage cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  2. Place eggs on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Toast eggs in the preheated oven until browned and fully crisp, about 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool down to room temperature.
